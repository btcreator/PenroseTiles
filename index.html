Random Penrose Pattern Generator beta

What is that?

Penrose tiling is a never repeating infinity pattern which got his name after Roger Penrose (mathematician and physicist) in the 1970s. For that aperiodic pattern you need just 2 different certain shapes, and follow some placement rules. For more information about what exactly a Penrose pattern is, please visit Wikipedia.

I think the most known tiles are romboids, but for that project I used an other variants, the Kite and Dart shapes. This generator generates an accurate random pattern from these. You have the possibility to adjust some properties of the tiling before generate, so feel free to play aroud.
