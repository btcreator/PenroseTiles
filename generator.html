Between two points can be drawn 4 different arcs. Which one gets drawn depends on the settings of the arc flags. The "Large arc" flag and the "Sweep" flag.

The Sweep flag swaps between the "inner" and "outer" arcs.
The Large arc flag swaps between the larger and smaller arc in the "inner" or "outer" group.
